Site Planning Document | WDD 231 Final Project

Site Name

The Green Thumb Directory

This name represents an authoritative yet accessible encyclopedia for indoor plants. "The Green Thumb" is a universally recognized idiom for gardening success, making the site instantly relatable to hobbyists.

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for indoor plant care by listing detailed growth requirements (light, water, soil) for various species, alongside a membership form for personalized care reminders and community updates.

Scenarios

  • "I have a north-facing apartment with very little natural light; which plants can survive in my home?"
  • "Where can I find a list of low-maintenance plants that are safe for pets?"

Color Scheme

The palette uses earthy greens to represent nature and a bright yellow accent to represent sunlight.

Primary: #1B4332
(Headers/Nav)
Secondary: #74C69D
(Buttons/Accents)
Accent: #FFB703
(CTAs/Icons)

Typography

Heading Font: Playfair Display (Serif) - Used for all major headings to provide a classic, botanical feel.

Body Font: Source Sans Pro (Sans-Serif) - Used for all paragraphs and data lists for maximum readability across devices.
